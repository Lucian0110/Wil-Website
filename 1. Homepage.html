Introduction

Empowering the Nation, established by Precious Radebe in 2018, specializes in offering high-quality training programs and courses tailored for domestic workers and gardeners across Johannesburg. Recognizing the importance of skill development for long-term employment and financial independence, we are dedicated to helping these workers expand their abilities and become more competitive in the workforce. With a focus on practical skills and empowerment, our programs cater specifically to individuals aiming to enhance their career opportunities or even start their own small businesses.

Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive skills enhancement and professional training that enable workers to secure stable employment, improve their quality of life, and contribute meaningfully to their communities. We believe that empowered workers lead to empowered families and communities.

Vision

Empowering the Nation envisions a future where domestic workers and gardeners are recognized not only for their hard work but also for their professionalism, skill, and potential as contributors to society. Our goal is to create a workforce that is highly skilled, respected, and capable of navigating a range of job opportunities. Through our training programs, we aim to open doors for these workers, enabling them to earn a fair wage, secure consistent employment, and even consider entrepreneurial ventures if they wish.
