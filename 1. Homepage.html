Introduction

Empowering the Nation established by Precious Radebe in 2018, specializes in providing online courses in Johannesburg for Domestic Workers and Gardeners. The training provided is used to enhance domestic workers and gardeners skills and ultimately making it easier for them to seek employment.

Mission

Our mission is to provide our workers with the best skills enhancement we can offer to eventually help them find employment much easier.

Vision

"Empowering the Nation aims to uplift and upskill domestic workers and gardeners, enhancing their employment prospects and entrepreneurial potential."
