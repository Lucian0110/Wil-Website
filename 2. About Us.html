About Us

Welcome to Empowering The Nation

We at Empowering the Nation believe that everyone should have the opportunity to flourish. Precious Radebe launched our business in 2018 with the goal of providing gardeners and domestic workers with the information and resources they need to advance their careers and embrace new opportunities.

Why We Were Founded

Empowering the Nation was born from a deep-seated need to support those who, like Preciousâ€™s own parents and family members, may not have had the same opportunities to gain formal training or upskilling. Understanding firsthand the limitations that many face due to a lack of resources, our founder saw the potential for a program that could help people transform their futures. Since 2018, our goal has been to bridge this gap and provide people with the chance to grow, learn, and thrive.

Impact on the Community

Beyond individual training, Empowering the Nation plays a role in strengthening communities. Each person we train has the potential to inspire others, contribute economically, and ultimately help reduce unemployment within the community. With every skilled worker, we create a ripple effect, spreading positive change that uplifts families, neighborhoods, and the greater Johannesburg area.

Core Values

  • Empowerment: Providing tools and knowledge that give workers confidence in their abilities.
  • Integrity: We maintain honesty and transparency in all our dealings with clients and partners.
  • Respect: We celebrate the dignity of every worker and respect their journey and experiences.
  • Community Impact: We strive to make a positive impact on the broader community by uplifting individuals.

What We Offer:

We offer a selection of courses that are tailored to our clients' specific requirements. Whether you are an employer looking to increase the skills of your domestic employees or a job seeker looking to advance your career for better prospects, we have a solution for you. Our programs include:

  • Short Term: Life skills, Child Minding, Cooking, Garden Maintenance.
  • 6 Months: First Aid, Sewing, Landscaping.
